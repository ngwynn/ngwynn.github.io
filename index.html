I made the stylus a couple of weeks ago because I didn't want to go out and buy one. It needs the cotton tip to be slightly wet to function. To make this I first looked up how an Ipad stylus works. The stylus conducts the heat from your hand since it needs the ipad to think you're touching it kind of. Without that it doesn't work, an example being if you've ever been outside and your hands are too cold to use your iphone. From there I just combined two ways to conduct energy metal (tin foil) and water (the slightly damp cotton tip). The tinfoil and the cotton must work for it to function. This could function with just the q-tip and the tin foil but to make it like a stylus and easy to use I took an old dried up ben and took out the insides before putting the q-tip in. It has no pressure sensitivity but was easy to make and works well.
